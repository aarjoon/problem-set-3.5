Tesla Model S

The Model S has a 5 star overall rating with great front-impact and side-impact protection. The car’s performance is amazing, with an acceleration of 0-60 mph in 2.5 seconds. This is an all-electric vehicle, providing many charging options with a maximum range of 335 miles. It is equipped with enhanced autopilot, using 12 ultrasonic sensors and a 360 degree view of all objects surrounding. The car provides seating up to 5 adults and 2 children and comes with a 17-inch touchscreen. The Model S was designed for speed and endurance that every single customer will enjoy.
