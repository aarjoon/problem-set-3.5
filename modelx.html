Tesla Model X

The Model X has a 5 star overall rating in safety. It is the safest SUV that has ever been built. There is room for everything for seating up to 7 adults and a towing capacity up to 5,000 pounds. This car comes with a range of up to 295 miles, allowing you to reach anywhere you want to go. The Model X comes with enhanced autopilot, using 12 ultrasonic sensors and rear, side, and forward-facing cameras allowing for a 360 degree view. This is the quickest SUV on the road, with an acceleration of 0-60 mph in 2.9 seconds. Maximum cargo space if provided with flat folding seats standard. The Model X is built for efficiency, making it the best vehicle in its class.
