Tesla Model 3

The Model 3 is the most affordable of all Tesla cars. A 5 star overall rating for safety with maximum strength in every area in the car. This car’s performance allows for an acceleration of 0-60 mph in 3.5 seconds. The all-wheel drive option comes with dual motors, independently controlling the front and back wheels. You will never need to visit a gas station again with this all-electric car, providing a range of 310 miles. The car is equipped with enhanced autopilot, providing a 360 degree view, 12 ultrasonic sensors, and a forward-facing radar to see objects 250 meters away. The car comes with a central 15-inch touchscreen and the ability to use your phone as a key. This affordable option will make your dream of owning a Tesla come true.
